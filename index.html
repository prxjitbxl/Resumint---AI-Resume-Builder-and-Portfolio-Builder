FAQ

What problem are you solving?

Students have skills and projects but struggle to present them in a way that reads like hiring signals. We convert raw notes into structured, impact-first resume + portfolio content.

How is it different from existing resume builders?

Most tools are template-first. We’re signal-first: we score and explain impact, clarity, and role fit, then generate content with consistent narrative across resume and portfolio.

What’s the AI part?

The core model can classify role intent, extract entities (tech, metrics, scope), and rewrite bullets with constraints (ATS-safe, quantified, non-buzzy). This landing demo simulates that flow.
