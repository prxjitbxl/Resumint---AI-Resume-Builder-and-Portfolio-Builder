## üöÄ About Resumint
ResuMint is a high-impact, AI-assisted **Resume & Portfolio Builder** designed to help students and professionals create "judge-impressive" profiles. It goes beyond simple formatting by focusing on **hiring signals**‚ÄîImpact, Ownership, and Tech Depth.

## ‚ú® Key Features
- **Signal-First Approach**: Maps your experience to key hiring signals instead of just listing responsibilities.
- **AI-Powered ATS Checker**: Real-time analysis of your resume for Application Tracking System (ATS) compatibility.
  - Checks for metrics, keywords, structure, and buzzwords.
  - Provides a "Clarity/Impact Score".
- **Interactive Portfolio Builder**: Generates a matching portfolio page directly from your resume profile.
- **Premium UI**: Features a "Mint Plasma" design with glassmorphism and subtle motion effects.
- **Local Storage**: Saves your profile data locally in your browser so you don't lose your work.
